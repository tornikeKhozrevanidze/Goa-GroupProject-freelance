Having a Skill or Profession

First of all, you need a specific skill or profession that clients can hire you for. For example:

• Design (logos, banners)

• Programming (websites, applications)

• Content writing (articles, blogs)

• Translation

• Video editing

• Digital marketing and more

Registering on a Platform or Finding Clients

Freelancers mostly work on specialized web platforms or find clients directly
Popular freelance platforms:

• Upwork.com

• Fiverr.com

• Freelancer.com

Selecting and Agreeing on a Project

After applying to a project, the client may contact you:

• To explain the details

• To request a sample or test

• To agree on price and deadline

Project Execution and Payment

After agreement, you start working:

• You work independently

• You regularly provide updates or final results to the client

• If necessary, you make revisions

After completing the project:

• On platforms, the payment is held in escrow and released after approval

• With direct clients, you agree on the payment method personally

Client Retention and Reputation

• If you work well, clients leave good reviews

• The higher your rating, the more clients trust you

• Successful freelancers often get contacted by new clients directly
